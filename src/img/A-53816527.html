Description

Keep your home organized in style with the Y-Weave Small Decorative Storage Basket from Brightroom™. It is functional and versatile to fit your every need; use it in any room to store keys, toiletries, accessories, and more. The storage basket is made from easy to maintain resin material with an elegant Y-Weave design, making it both durable and stylish, the perfect alternative to wicker baskets. It is perfect for any room, matches with any décor style and is ideal for use in open spaces such as shelving or table surfaces. The plastic construction offers sturdy, lightweight convenience, and the color and design easily coordinate with the rest of your decor giving you a functional accent you can easily add to just about any room in your home.

Brightroom™ started with a simple truth: When you’ve got it together, you feel better. Our versatile designs reveal the possibility in every closet, cupboard and drawer, and work together to create brilliant ways to organize your every day.
